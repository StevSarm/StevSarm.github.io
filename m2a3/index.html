Come explore Stampings National Park! Stampings National Park is ideal for getting a good look at the mountain range from various scenic trails. While you're there, there will be plenty of opportunites to see wildlife such as deer, moose, fish, bats, and more! We offer canoe rides, campgrounds and a history museum. More events are planned throughout the year.
