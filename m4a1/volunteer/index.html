Volunteer with Us

Support your community by helping us maintain our park!

Stampings National Park offers various volunteer programs with a diverse selection of opportunities. Some opportunities require specific licenses, certifications, knowledge, and skills, but there are still many positions open for anyone willing to help.

Guide Positions

  • Trail Guide
  • History Walk Guide
  • Sunset Stroll Guide
  • Sunrise Gathering Guide

Cleaning Positions

  • Trail Cleanup
  • River Cleanup
  • Trash Gathering

Please visit our offices on-site to apply for any positions.
